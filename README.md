## Who am I and what am I doing here?

- I’m a self-taught data analyst working in the field of fisheries socioeconomic analyses. Think studying what a particular fishing fleet catches, when, to which port is it landed, at what price and who are the people involved in this work.
- I use R, Excel and Tableau to help me dig through thousand of rows of data on catches, fishing vessel characteristics and crew demographics.
- I’m currently looking to formalise and consolidate everything I have learnt over these years about data analysis and visualisation. This profile is one of the tools I am exploring for this purpose. Also here to showcase my work :)
- There may also be a bit of exploration on other topics than fisheries. The world is a big place with many interesting areas after all.
- As you may guess from the above picture, I am currently based in Scotland (not in the Highlands though. I wish).
